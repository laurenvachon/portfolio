Photographs

A collection of some of my work

Zip, zero, nil, nada - the amount of photography knowledge I had before purchasing my first real camera. The result was hundreds of hours reading blogs, watching tutorials on youtube, thousands of blurry, under & over exposed photos, and failing miserably over and over again.

5 years later, I'm starting to get the hang of it...

My style is organic. I prefer to work with natural light, usually in the morning during the golden hour. On more than one occasion, I've stumbled upon the night owls that are still enjoying their night out as the sun is rising.

My favorite photographs are the ones that capture someone or something in their true form. Nowadays, I like to think of myself of a professional holiday card photographer. I'm always willing to get up at the crack of dawn to catch a sunrise, or skip out on dinner to catch a sunset.
