Photographs

Zip, zero, nil, nada - the amount of photography knowledge I had before purchasing my first real camera. The result was hundreds of hours reading blogs, watching tutorials on youtube, thousands of blurry, under & over exposed photos, and failing miserably over and over again.

5 years later, I'm starting to get the hang of it...

My style is organic, creative. My favorite photographs are the ones that capture someone or something in their true form. Nowadays, I like to think of myself of a professional holiday card photographer. I'm always down to get up at the crack of dawn to catch a sunrise, or skip out on dinner to catch a sunset.

My passion for traveling and new experiences has taken me to unique places that I've gotten to explore with my camera.
