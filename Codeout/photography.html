Photography

A collection of some of my work

Zip, zero, nil, nada - the amount of photography knowledge I had before purchasing my first real camera. The result was hundreds of hours reading blogs, watching tutorials on youtube, thousands of blurry, under & over exposed photos, and failing miserably over and over again.

5 years later, I'm starting to get the hang of it...

What I love most about photography is how it allows me to travel and show people how I see the world.

I love that a photograph can evoke a feeling, a memory, a sound, and so much more. In all of the photos I take, I try to portray the moment exactly as it was so that it can be relived a thousand times over.

I'm always exploring and finding new places to shoot. On the weekend, you can find me walking around Boston at sunrise, or catching a sunset on the beach.
