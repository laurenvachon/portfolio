MX New England App Concept

UI/UX Design.

Sketch | Mobile App | Concept

Created March 2017

MX New England was created to help motocross riders in the New England area find events and tracks to ride at. After designing their website, I decided to make this concept app that explored the idea a bit further, by adding registration, event results, and further integration with your mobile device.

View Invision Prototype

Home screen that gives an overview of the most important information for racers: notifications, upcoming events & results.

Track list using Material design principles.

Notification screen, to be used for urgent notifications such as delays, cancellations, etc.
