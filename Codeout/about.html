About Me

I'm Lauren Vachon, a web designer and front end web developer from Wayland, MA. I am a proud Quinnipiac Bobcat where I earned my degree in Computer Information Systems and minored in Interactive Digital Design. I like to think of myself as being both left and right brained, the perfect marriage of logic & creativity. I pride myself on being able to hand code. I am very easy to work with, a fast learner, and am self motivated. When I'm not designing and coding I can be found with a camera in my hand.

Hey there, I'm Lauren and I design for screens. I started my journey at Quinnipac University in Hamden, CT where I studied Computer Information Systems. I got my start at MembersFirst, working as an interactive designer.
