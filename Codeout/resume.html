Hey there, I'm Lauren and I'm a vegetarian, compulsive Mean Girls/Bridesmaids/Pitch Perfect quoter, and teller of bad jokes.

Growing up, I dreamed of being a marine biologist, a carpenter (like my dad), and a professional basketball player. I know what you're thinking - I couldn't have ended up any further from my childhood dreams.

My report cards have always said: "Hard worker and very chatty." Little has changed.

I went to college at Quinnipac University in Hamden, CT where I studied Computer Information Systems. At the end of my sophomore year, I took a Digital Design course and fell in love with interface design. The combination of the thought behind design and the ability to make things move and interact just made sense and things started to click.

Nowadays, when I'm not busy solving problems with design, I'm exploring Boston or some new place with my camera. Currently working as a Senior Interactive Designer, Team Lead at MembersFirst.
