2017 Interactive Media Award Winner
Best in Class - Lifestyle

Layered Design | Lifestyle | Public & Private

Launched May 2016

Located in Honolulu, this business club's breathtaking views and flawless service peg it as the premier destination for business meetings, special events and downtown pau hana gatherings. This is a special club and needed a lifestyle-inspired redesign of their website to compliment.

The goals called for a fresh, bright and open site redesign to rebrand their club as one of the leading city clubs in Honolulu. MembersFirst built a modern site that focuses on large photography, is more simplistic overall, and easier to navigate. To achieve this, we used a mixture of modern fonts with a contemporary, magazine-like layout.

Being that the club is in Hawaii, they needed a site that told their story through photos, while also not being too long to scroll. Palm trees and tropical leaves are a facet of the design to help cement that “Hawaiian Club” feel, giving the overall feeling of the site a light, airy, and breathable quality.
