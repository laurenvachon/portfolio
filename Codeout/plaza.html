Best in Class - Lifestyle

Layered Design | Lifestyle | Public & Private

Launched May 2016

Located in Honolulu, this business club's breathtaking views and flawless service make it the premier destination for business meetings, special events and downtown pau hana gatherings. Since it is such a unique club, it needed a lifestyle-inspired website to compliment it.

The goals called for a fresh, bright and open site redesign to rebrand the club as one of the leading city clubs in Honolulu. I built a modern site that focuses on large photography, simplicity, and ease of use. To achieve this, I used a mixture of sans-serif fonts with a contemporary, magazine-like layout.

Being that the club is in a picturesque setting, it needed a design that told its story through photos, while minimizing scroll. Palm trees and tropical leaves are a facet of the design to help cement that “Hawaiian Club” feel, giving the overall sense of a light, airy, and breathable experience.
